About OopBuy Spreadsheet

OopBuy Spreadsheet is an independent buyer research resource for people who want a cleaner way to organize finds, compare categories, and prepare for QC review before shipping.

Our Focus

The site focuses on practical buying decisions: what to inspect, how to think about sizing, when to request measurements, and how shipping weight can affect the final cost of a haul.

Independent Resource

This site is not affiliated with OopBuy. It is designed as a planning guide for shoppers researching agent workflows and category options.
